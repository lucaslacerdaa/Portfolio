Introduction

Understand more about the project

This is a landmark project in my career as a Product Designer. It involved launching an MVP for a new bank card while I was still working as a software developer. This opportunity allowed me to showcase my skills in creating product proposals, mastery of Figma software and aesthetic understanding. Throughout the project, I learned the importance of empathy with the user and aligning aesthetics with the brand identity. This meaningful experience was a turning point in my career, and I hope to inspire other professionals seeking a similar path.
It is important to remember that this project is a case study, with no financial commitment.

A little bit of Marketing

I needed to spark emotion in my recruiter's eyes, even if this product wasn't officially launched. It was crucial to create expectations aligned with the company's needs, and I nailed it. The eyes of the person on the other side of the screen shone, and I was immensely happy... to this day that memory remains vivid. I had no idea that this would be my first delivery of value as a product designer :)
